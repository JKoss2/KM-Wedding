Parking?

Ceremony / Corpus Christi Catholic Church:

Corpus Christi church will have plenty of free parking available.

Reception / Gaylord Palms:

The Gaylord Palms Resort has two (2) options for parking:
Self-Parking (FREE w/ validation): You can park in the "Key West" garage/lot. When exiting, you will need to VALIDATE YOUR PARKING BY PRESENTING YOUR VOUCHER!
An announcement will be made regarding the vouchers during the reception.
Valet ($35): Valet at the front of the resort for the discounted rate of $35 for the day.
